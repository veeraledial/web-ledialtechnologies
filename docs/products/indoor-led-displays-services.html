Subcategory

Fixed Installation LED Display

Fixed Installation LED Displays are permanent visual solutions designed for long-term use in specific venues. Unlike rental or portable displays, these are integrated directly into the building's architecture.

Highlights

  • Permanent Integration: They are typically bolted to a wall, recessed into a surface, or mounted on a heavy-duty permanent steel structure.
  • Seamless Video Walls: These displays are made of multiple small modules that fit together perfectly, creating a single, massive screen without any visible lines or "bezels" between panels.

Common Applications

  • Corporate Lobbies: Used for high-impact branding and welcoming guests with large-scale visuals.
  • Control Rooms: Providing high-resolution data visualization for security, traffic, or utility monitoring.

Subcategory

LED Poster Displays

LED Poster Displays, often called Digital LED Posters or iPosters, are slim, freestanding screens designed to replace traditional static roll-up banners.

Highlights

  • Plug-and-Play: They are incredibly user-friendly, allowing content to be updated via USB, Wi-Fi, or HDMI without complex setup.
  • Highly Portable: These displays are lightweight and often come with built-in wheels or a kickstand, making them easy to move around a venue.

Common Applications

  • Retail & Malls: Perfect for showcasing seasonal sales, new arrivals, or high-end luxury branding at store entrances.
  • Events & Trade Shows: Used as digital information boards or directional signage that can be updated in real-time.

Subcategory

Flexible LED displays

Flexible LED displays are a creative category of screens made from soft, pliable materials like flexible PCB (Printed Circuit Board) or rubber. This allows them to be bent, folded, or curved into complex shapes that traditional rigid panels cannot achieve.

Highlights

  • High Flexibility: They can form concave or convex curves, cylinders, waves, and other artistic designs.
  • Lightweight and Thin: Their specialized construction makes them much lighter and thinner than standard LED cabinets, simplifying installation on curved surfaces.

Common Applications

  • Architectural Features: Wrapping around circular columns in malls or airports.
  • Stage Design: Creating wave-like backdrops or curved immersive environments for concerts and studios.

Subcategory

Fine pitch and small pixel pitch LED display

Fine pitch and small pixel pitch LED displays are characterized by their high pixel density, which allows for sharp, clear images even when viewed from a very short distance.

Highlights

  • Small Pixel Pitch: These displays typically have a pixel pitch of less than 2.5mm (e.g., P0.9, P1.2, P1.5).
  • High Resolution: Due to the tight spacing between LEDs, these screens can easily achieve 4K and 8K resolutions.

Common Applications

  • Command and control centers
  • Corporate boardrooms
