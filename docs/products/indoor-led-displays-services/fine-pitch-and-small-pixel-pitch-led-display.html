Indoor LED Displays Services

Fine pitch and small pixel pitch LED display

Fine pitch and small pixel pitch LED displays are characterized by their high pixel density, which allows for sharp, clear images even when viewed from a very short distance.

Highlights

  • Small Pixel Pitch: These displays typically have a pixel pitch of less than 2.5mm (e.g., P0.9, P1.2, P1.5).
  • High Resolution: Due to the tight spacing between LEDs, these screens can easily achieve 4K and 8K resolutions.
  • Seamless Design: Unlike traditional LCD video walls, fine pitch LED displays are modular and can be assembled into any size or shape without visible bezels or gaps.
  • High Performance: They offer high refresh rates (up to 7680Hz) for flicker-free visuals and high grayscale for superior color expression even at low brightness.
  • Versatile Applications: Common uses include command and control centers, corporate boardrooms, broadcast studios, and luxury retail environments.

Common Applications

  • Command and control centers
  • Corporate boardrooms
  • Broadcast studios
  • Luxury retail environments
